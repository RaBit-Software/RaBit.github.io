Packages

The Me, Myself, and I Package

With this multi-dimensional package, we offer an array of photography and videography services, including, but not limited to, personal photography sessions for whatever the occasion may be (or simply just because!), custom professional headshots, full body shots in the attire of your choice, video shorts for your business, and so forth. The possibilities are endless. You tell us your vision, and we work directly with you to bring it to life!

The Wedding Package

You and your future spouse have the opportunity to be our very first wedding clients! When my wife and I got married, we hired a photographer, but were disappointed with the quality of our photos. We know first hand how important this day is for our couples! Our goal is to provide stunning, authentic, and captivating wedding photographs that you will cherish for a lifetime. We would be honored to capture the best day of your life! We also offer a simultaneous videography service shot in 4k resolution, complete with editing and a highlight reel!

The Matriarch Package

With this package, we offer *breathtaking* maternity photoshoots, as well as photoshoots of our mama bears with their precious children, taken in a way that captures the essence of a true Matriarch.

The Power Couple Package

We offer a unique service known as our "power couple" package. With this package, we coordinate with you to bring your vision to life, and to portray you and your significant other in a "powerful" light. We will even coordinate with you in terms of outfits/style/makeup, location, and the overall tone/theme!
